About Us
The journey begins in 2008 with the magical ambition to explore the dreamland of kids on earth. We started with the two paint brushes holding by two wonderful people with the goal to bright up every faces through face painting, the Creative creation of such people led the Kids Dream to this far in the magical land.

Now we offering Balloon Twisting, Magic show and much more magical entertainment to bright up with loads of fun to any kinds of event such as personal events to corporate events, Birthday and Opening ceremonies.

We are not the outlaw in the Magical Land of Earth, rather we are the responsible team with our effort and equipment for our works. We use the best in the market materials for our face painting and skin related materials. Under one umbrella, you will find face painter, balloon Twister, Magician, costume Entertainer, Henna Artist, and Event Planner. We wish to be the part of an amazing event for you and your Kids while you can sit back and relax enjoying the show.
